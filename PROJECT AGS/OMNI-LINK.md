# Aegis Project: Omni-Link Module Catalog
**Compatibility:** P.S.O. Sentinel / U.C. Juggernaut / Vanguard Class
**Interface:** Universal Hardpoint (Shoulder, Forearm, Back-Mount)

---

## 1. Primary Weapon Systems (Forearm/Hand-Held)
* **ARC-9 Railgun (Standard U.C. Issue):** A high-velocity magnetic accelerator that draws directly from the O-Cell's peak discharge. Capable of piercing through three layers of reinforced ORI-alloy.
* **P.S.O. Suppression Baton:** A massive, non-lethal kinetic energy weapon designed for riot control. It emits an ultrasonic pulse that disables electronics and disorients hostile infantry.
* **"Cinder" Thermal Blade:** A Vanguard-specific melee module. The edge is superheated using O-Cell thermal waste, allowing it to slice through enemy mech chassis like butter.

## 2. Defensive Modules (Shoulder/Back)
* **Aegis Kinetic Shield:** A deployable energy barrier. Uses 15% O-Cell capacity per minute of active use. The AI can manually adjust the shield's frequency to block specific projectile types.
* **APS (Active Protection System):** A shoulder-mounted automated turret that intercepts incoming missiles or rockets before they impact the main frame.
* **Smoke/Chaff Discharger:** Creates a multispectral cloud that masks the mech from infrared and radar detection—essential for Vanguard units.

## 3. Utility & Tactical Modules (Back/External)
* **Vector-Link Relay:** An antenna array that boosts Intelligence signal, allowing the mech to act as a mobile command hub for smaller PFLD drones or local P.S.O. squads.
* **Jump-Kit Thrusters:** High-output boosters attached to the back and legs. Allows for short bursts of flight or "dash maneuvers" to dodge heavy fire.
* **Emergency O-Cell Reserves:** A secondary, smaller rack that holds two backup O-Cells. When the primary hits 0%, the AI can initiate a Hot-Swap without the pilot exiting the cockpit.

## 4. Specialized Integrated Tech
* **Deep-Scan Radar:** ORI-developed sonar adapted for land. Can see through up to 50 feet of concrete or debris to find hidden targets.
* **The "Vulture" Recovery Claw:** A specialized forearm module for P.S.O. recovery teams to lift heavy debris or extract compromised pilot pods from the field.
