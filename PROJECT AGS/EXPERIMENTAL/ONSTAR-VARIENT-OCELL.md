# ⚠️ INTERNAL MEMORANDUM: PROJECT SOL-CELL (SCRAPPED)
**Project Codename:** OnStar-Variant O-Cell  
**Principal Investigator:** Parafield Corporation R&D  
**Subject:** Integration of S.F.R. Mark II Energy into Portable Cell Architecture  
**Status:** ABORTED - ALL DATA ARCHIVED - EXPERIMENTAL UNITS DECOMMISSIONED  

---

## 1. Concept Summary
Project SOL-CELL was an experimental venture to see if the **Synthetic Fusion Reactor Mark II (S.F.R. / Project OnStar)** could be miniaturized and stabilized within a standard Orange Corp O-Cell casing. The goal was to replace the standard chemical catalyst with a **harnessable artificial star fragment**, creating a near-infinite power loop for Aegis-class units.

## 2. Technical Divergence
Unlike the standard cerulean O-Cells, the SOL-CELL prototype exhibited a violent **amber-yellow/orange glow**, a visual byproduct of the contained fusion reaction.

* **Energy Density:** Theoretical ∞ (limited only by casing integrity).
* **Performance Gain:** Estimated 1,200% increase in thruster output and shield density.
* **Visual Signature:** High-intensity solar flares contained within the tungsten-glass housing.

## 3. The "OnStar" Instability
During the final testing phase, several catastrophic flaws were identified that led to the immediate termination of the project:

### Thermal Criticality
Standard Aegis cooling systems could not compensate for the S.F.R. waste heat. The experimental O-Cell reached temperatures exceeding 5,000°C within minutes of high-torque combat simulation, threatening to melt the chassis from the inside out.

### Neural Feedback (Vector Interference)
The Vector-Class AI reacted violently to the SOL-CELL. The "Star" energy caused massive "static" in the neural link, leading the AI to experience symptoms of **Digital Psychosis**. Pilots reported the AI screaming or becoming hyper-aggressive as the fusion energy began to "overload" the machine's personality matrix.

### Catastrophic Risk
Unlike standard O-Cells which vent energy safely upon a breach, a SOL-CELL breach would result in a localized fusion detonation. A single destroyed Vanguard unit would possess the explosive yield of a tactical nuclear strike.

## 4. Final Directive
By order of Parafield Executive Leadership, the **SOL-CELL** is deemed a violation of Security Protocols and DANGEROUS for operational use. 
* **Standard Blue O-Cells** remain the only authorized power source for P.S.O. and U.C. Military operations.
* All existing amber prototypes have been submerged in lead-lined cooling vats within the PFLD "Deep Vault."
* Project OnStar will remain a stationary power grid solution only.
