# TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION: JENSON SPACE STATION (JSS)
### **Project:** Sovereign-Class Strategic Defense Platform
### **Operator:** United Columbus (U.C.) Military / Parafield Security Operations (P.S.O.)
### **Finalized Date:** September 2074

### I. OVERVIEW
The Jenson Space Station (JSS) represents the pinnacle of human engineering and orbital hegemony as of 2070. Commissioned by the Columbian Empire and constructed by Parafield, the JSS serves as the primary defensive "Queen Pawn" for the SOL Sector. It is designed to act as a permanent deterrent and a mobile fortress capable of interstellar intervention. Began construction in the 2060's and finalized and entered operational status in 2074.

## II. CORE SPECIFICATIONS
* **Dimensions:** 42km (Length) x 18km (Diameter)
* **Mass:** 1.2 Billion Metric Tons
* **Cost:** Approximately 450 Trillion (During 2070)
* **Crew Capacity:** 250,000 Total
* **Primary Power Plant:** Synthetic Fusion Reactor (S.F.R.) Mark II
    * **Codename:** Project OnStar
    * **Description:** A contained artificial star providing near-infinite energy for propulsion, structural integrity fields, and high-energy weaponry.

## III. TACTICAL ARMAMENTS (FTL DEPLOYMENT LOADOUT)
These systems are authorized for use when the JSS utilizes FTL drives to jump into active combat theaters where no other space stations are available to provide a strategic advantage.

### 1. Solar-Flare Lancer (SFL)
A kinetic-energy accelerator that draws superheated "star-matter" from the S.F.R. Mark II.
* **Mechanism:** Plasma is siphoned into a magnetic containment field and fired at 0.1% the speed of light.
* **Tactical Application:** Penetration of Dreadnought-class hulls and moon-sized celestial bodies.

### 2. Orbital Distortion Field
A hybrid defensive-offensive gravitational projection system.
* **Mechanism:** Generates a massive gravitational well that bends incoming energy and kinetic projectiles around the station.
* **Tactical Application:** Allows the JSS to "anchor" in the center of enemy formations, using gravitational shear to crush escort ships.

### 3. Stellar Beam Array (The Cutter)
A continuous-wave fusion energy beam.
* **Mechanism:** Uses the station’s massive radiator fins as focusing lenses to sweep raw fusion energy across a field of fire.
* **Tactical Application:** Rapid bisection of entire carrier groups or fleet-wide area denial.

### IV. THE OMEGA PROTOCOL: STELLAR SINGULARITY LANCE
The ultimate weapon of the Columbian Empire, used only when the total destruction of a planetary body is mandated.

* **Mechanism:** The JSS projects a high-density dark-matter needle into the target’s core to create a gravitational anchor. Unfiltered S.F.R. plasma is then injected directly into the planet.
* **Lethality Options:**
    1.  **Planetary Fragmentation:** Thermal expansion exceeds the target’s gravitational binding energy, shattering the planet.
    2.  **Singularity Collapse:** Reversing magnetic polarity compresses the planet’s mass into its Schwarzschild Radius, creating a conventional black hole.
* **UNAVAILABLITY WARNING:** after use, the JSS will immediately FTL back to the SOL Sector for repairs. Approx time until repairs are done are around 2 weeks at best.
* **Authorization Requirement (Tri-Key):**
    1.  Imperial High Command (Political Mandate)
    2.  U.C. Military General Staff (Tactical Necessity)
    3.  Parafield Executive Board (S.F.R. Bypass Codes)

* **Key Caveats:**
    * Use of the Singularity Lance results in a 95% energy drain on the S.F.R. Mark II. And potential damages may occur.
    * Post-firing "Reaction Kickback" requires immediate FTL egress to avoid gravitational instability.
    * Massive cooling periods are required following FTL jumps to maintain structural hull integrity.
